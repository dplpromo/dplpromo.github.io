About This Visualization

This visualization displays global temperature anomalies from 1880 to 2022 based on the NOAA Global Surface Temperature Dataset (NOAAGlobalTemp). The data shows temperature anomalies relative to the 1971-2000 baseline period.

The charts show:

  • Annual Temperature Anomalies: Yearly temperature differences from the baseline, with a 5-year moving average to show the trend more clearly.
  • Decadal Averages: Average temperature anomalies for each decade, showing the progressive warming trend.
  • Custom Range Explorer: Allows you to select a specific time period to examine in more detail.

The data clearly shows a warming trend over the past century, with a significant acceleration in recent decades. The trend per decade and total warming since pre-industrial times are key indicators of climate change.

Data Source: NOAA Global Surface Temperature Dataset
